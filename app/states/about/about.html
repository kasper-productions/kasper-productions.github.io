About Us

KASPer (Korean & American Student Performers) is an organization of University of Wisconsin - Madison that is split into into a dance crew and a production crew.

KASPer Productions are the creative minds of KASPer. We are responsible for all photography and videography, and comprised of talented individuals with a variety of specialities.
